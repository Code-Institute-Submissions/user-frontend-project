About

Edward Christopher Sheeran, also know as, Ed Sheeran (born 17 February 1991) who was brought up in Halifax, West Yorkshire. He is known for being songwriter, guitarist, record producer, and even an actor. 2012 was the year where had set his mark as he had won the Brit awards for Best British Male Solo Artist and British Breakthrough Act. Ed Sheeran has won over a hundred awards throughout his career so far. Most notably winning four Grammy awards which were for, Song of the Year, Best Pop Solo Performance (x2) and Best Pop Vocal Album. Alongside Ed Sheerans awards, his debut album, + (pronounced "plus"), was released in September 2011. It topped the UK and Australian charts, reached number five in the US, and has since been certified eight-times platinum in the UK.
